• When scheduling the call or getting contacted by the volunteer be clear about what you need help with. You need to write down, in no less than 250 characters, what the meeting will be about.
  • If you don't write about what the meeting is about the volunteer can ignore the request and don't show up for the meeting.
    This helps us to ensure that you are committed to it.
  • Arrive on time for the meeting.
  • If you can't make it to the meeting, please let the volunteer know it in advance.
  • Be polite.
  • Try to be prepared before the call and, if possible, have a list of questions that you want to ask.
  • Volunteers can reject a call.
  • A call with a stranger can be intimidated at first, volunteers will do their best to make you feel comfortable during the call.
  • If you have a bad experience please contact me
  • If you have a great experience share it with friends or leave a message in our telegram channel and group
